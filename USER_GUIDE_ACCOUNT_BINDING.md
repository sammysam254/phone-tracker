# 📱 Parental Control - Account Binding Guide

## 🎯 Quick Start (3 Steps!)

### Step 1: Create Parent Account
1. Go to the web dashboard: `https://your-dashboard.com`
2. Click "Create Parent Account"
3. Enter your email and password
4. Verify your email (check inbox)

### Step 2: Install Child App
1. Download the child app on your child's device
2. Install and open the app
3. You'll see the login screen

### Step 3: Bind Device
1. On the child device, tap "Login & Bind Device"
2. Enter YOUR parent account email and password
3. Grant consent when prompted
4. Grant permissions (calls, SMS, location, etc.)
5. Done! Device is now bound to your account

## 📊 View Activities

1. Go to web dashboard: `https://your-dashboard.com`
2. Login with your parent account
3. Your bound devices will appear automatically
4. Select a device to view activities

## 🔄 Bind Multiple Devices

Want to monitor multiple children's devices?

1. Install child app on each device
2. Login with YOUR parent account on each device
3. All devices will appear in your dashboard
4. Each device will have a unique name (e.g., "Samsung Galaxy A52")

## 🔓 Unbind a Device

Need to remove a device from monitoring?

1. Open child app on the device
2. Tap "Unbind Device" button
3. Confirm unbinding
4. Device will be removed from your dashboard

## 🆘 Troubleshooting

### "Invalid email or password"
- Double-check your email and password
- Make sure you're using your PARENT account credentials
- Try resetting your password on the web dashboard

### "Device binding failed"
- Check internet connection on child device
- Make sure you have a verified parent account
- Try logging out and back in

### "Device doesn't appear in dashboard"
- Wait 30 seconds and refresh the dashboard
- Make sure you completed the consent step
- Check that the child device has internet connection

### "Activities not showing"
- Make sure you granted all permissions
- Check that monitoring service is running (green indicator)
- Verify device has internet connection

## 🔐 Security & Privacy

### Your Data is Safe
- All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
- Only you can access your child's activities
- No one else can see your data (not even us!)

### Account Security
- Use a strong, unique password
- Don't share your account credentials
- Enable two-factor authentication (if available)

### Child Privacy
- Always get consent before monitoring
- Explain to your child why you're monitoring
- Respect their privacy and trust

## ❓ FAQ

### Q: Can I use the same account on multiple devices?
**A:** Yes! Log into the child app on each device with your parent account.

### Q: What happens if I change my password?
**A:** Your bound devices will continue working. You'll just use the new password to bind new devices.

### Q: Can I unbind and rebind a device?
**A:** Yes! Unbind the device, then log in again to rebind it.

### Q: Do I need the parent app?
**A:** No! The parent app is no longer needed. Just use the web dashboard.

### Q: What if I forget my password?
**A:** Use the "Forgot Password" link on the web dashboard to reset it.

### Q: Can multiple parents monitor the same device?
**A:** No, each device can only be bound to one parent account at a time.

### Q: What permissions are required?
**A:** 
- Phone calls (to monitor call logs)
- SMS (to monitor text messages)
- Location (to track device location)
- Camera (for remote camera access)
- Microphone (for remote audio)
- Accessibility (for screen monitoring)
- Device Admin (for remote lock)

### Q: Is this legal?
**A:** Yes, as long as:
- You're the parent/legal guardian
- The child is a minor
- You get consent from the child
- You comply with local laws
